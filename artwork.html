Chaos and Coffee Stains

an explosion of vibrant colors swirling in a playful dance. Amidst the bold strokes, you might spot the outline of a coffee cup, a nod to the artist’s caffeine-fueled creativity. This piece invites viewers to find order in the chaos and ponder: "Was coffee really spilled here?"

About the artist

Meet Pat "The Splatter Master" Johnson, a virtuoso of vibrant disarray. Pat’s artistic journey began with an accidental paint spill, leading to masterpieces that blend bold creativity with a dash of humor. Known for their unpredictable style, Pat loves explaining their work and assuring bewildered audiences, "Yes, it’s supposed to look like that."
